About

Care4U is focused on developing next-generation treatments for cardiovascular diseases. Our strategy is to develop living tissue replacements using regenerative engineering and biomedical nanotechnology approaches. Such living tissues have the capacity for remodeling, repair, and growth and offer substantial safety, efficacy, and durability advantages compared to current treatment options
